How to Use Vaultless

Secure, deterministic password generation — your way.

Using Vaultless Without an Account

Generate secure passwords instantly without creating an account. Your master password is never stored or transmitted.

1

Enter a service name (e.g., "Gmail" or "Bank Account")

2

Enter your master password (never stored or transmitted)

3

Choose desired length (8–64 characters)

4

Click Generate to get your unique password

With an Account (Recommended)

Create an account to enhance security and personalization. Your passwords become uniquely tied to your identity.

1

Register or login to generate your personal key

2

Your passwords become personalized and unique to your account

3

Even with same inputs, different users get different results

Pro Tips

  • Use different names per service (e.g., "Gmail", "Netflix", "Banking")
  • Remember your master password — it is never stored or transmitted
  • Self-host Vaultless using our Docker guide for full control
  • Use our browser extension for seamless integration with websites
